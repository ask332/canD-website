The first of driving license in the form of NFTs. These will be minted using a program that will rate the driving skills of an individual. Based on the score of the individual, the program the drivers will be categorized into three groups. These NFTs will have a time-bound validity. For the first time driving licenses will be issued autonomously and without a central entity.

We intend to make these NFTs valid globally by developing software that can score the driving skills of an individual anywhere around the globe.

These license will of three types:

canD Level 1

Drivers having having highest score.

canD Level 2

Drivers with average score.

canD Level 3

Newbies who just learnt how to drive.
